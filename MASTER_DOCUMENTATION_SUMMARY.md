### 🛠️ Installation Guidelines (Stone + Metal)

#### **Critical Requirements:**
1. ✅ Concrete posts MUST cure **72 hours** before metal installation
2. ✅ Use **stainless steel or galvanized fasteners ONLY**
3. ✅ Rebar must extend through **full height** of post
4. ✅ Expansion joints every **20-30 feet**
5. ✅ Dissimilar metals require **isolation gaskets** (prevent corrosion)

#### **Tools Required:**
- Masonry drill with carbide bits
- Torque wrench (10-100 ft-lbs)
- 4ft level minimum
- Angle grinder (metal cutting)
- Welding equipment (if required)
- Safety equipment (gloves, glasses, steel-toe boots)

#### **Safety Notes:**
- ⚠️ Metal panels have sharp edges - heavy gloves required
- ⚠️ Lift heavy panels with 2+ workers
- ⚠️ Check for overhead power lines
- ⚠️ Ground welding equipment properly
- ⚠️ Wear welding helmet & protective clothing
