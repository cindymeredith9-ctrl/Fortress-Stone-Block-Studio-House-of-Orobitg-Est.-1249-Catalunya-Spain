# 📐 HOW TO FIND THE BLUEPRINT DESIGNER (AutoCAD Export)

## **METHOD 1: HOME SCREEN - PROFESSIONAL TOOLS SECTION** ⭐ EASIEST!

### **STEP-BY-STEP:**

1. **Open the app** and go to **Home Screen**
   
2. **Scroll down** past the featured products
   
3. **Look for the PROFESSIONAL TOOLS section** (about halfway down)

4. **Find these TWO cards side-by-side:**

   **📐 Blueprint Designer** (Gray/Slate Card)
   - Icon: Ruler 📏

   - Button: "Draw Plans"

   - Icon: Document 📄

## **WHAT YOU'LL FIND IN BLUEPRINT DESIGNER:**

### **Drawing Tools:**
- ✅ **Select/Move** - Select and reposition elements
- ✅ **Wall** - Draw thick structural walls (8px)
- ✅ **Door** - Add 3-foot swing doors
- ✅ **Window** - Add 4-foot windows
- ✅ **Rectangle** - Draw room outlines
- ✅ **Circle** - Add circular features
- ✅ **Dimension** - Add measurement lines with arrows
- ✅ **Text** - Add labels and notes

### **Professional Features:**
- ✅ **Grid System** - 1-foot and 5-foot grid lines
- ✅ **Snap to Grid** - Precision alignment
- ✅ **Rulers** - Horizontal and vertical (in feet)
- ✅ **Custom Dimensions** - Set project width × length

### **Export Options:**
- ✅ **Export to PDF** - Professional quality, print-ready
- ✅ **Export to DWG/DXF** - Opens in AutoCAD!
- ✅ **Save Project** - Save for later editing
- ✅ **Professional Title Blocks** - House of Orobitg™ branding
